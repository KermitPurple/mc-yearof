SERVICE

2009-2010

Over the years, the “Year Of” program has provided a framework for looking at the relationship between science and religion, humans and our environments, the relationships between self and society, methods of conflict transformation, and – this year – service.

Moorpark College has dedicated 2009–2010 academic year to being the Year of Service, in order to:

  • Recognize the service demonstrated by students and employees of the college,

  • Provide opportunities for volunteer service to the local community, to the nation, and to the world,

  • Encourage an attitude of service — an approach to life that integrates understanding, caring, and taking action — that carries on in the lives of our campus community beyond this single year.

You may contact The Year of Service via email.
