DEMOCRACY

Over the years, the “Year Of” program has provided a framework for looking at the relationship between science and religion, humans and our environments, the relationships between self and society, methods of conflict transformation, and – this year – democracy.

This Year of Democracy is a series of speakers and events clustered around the theme of Democracy that are designed to engage the campus community in conversation and activities that enlighten, inform and inspire.
