ECONOMY

2010-2011

Over the years, the “Year Of” program has provided a framework for looking at the relationship between science and religion, humans and our environments, the relationships between self and society, methods of conflict transformation, and – this year – the economy.

The theme for Moorpark College’s Academic Year 2010-2011 is the Year of the Economy.

The co-chairs for this year are Rachel Messinger (Anthropology) and Brendan Purdy (Mathematics).
