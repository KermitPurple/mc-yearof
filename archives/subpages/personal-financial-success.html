Lecture by Tim Weaver

In today’s world, credit is integrated into everyday life. From paying for college education, renting a car, reserving an airline ticket, or hotel room, credit cards have become a necessary convenience. However, using credit wisely is critical to building a solid credit a general idea about the advantages and disadvantages of credit, this lecture provides an opportunity to discuss these issues in more detail, as well as, establishing a personal budget to manage resources. Young people and others commonly wonder how to establish credit. During the lecture, students will learn about the creditworthiness factors of character, capital, and capacity in order to help them gain an understanding of how to start and maintain of credit: single-payment credit, installment credit, and revolving credit. Finally, this lecture will educate students about how much credit might be appropriate for their situation.

For more information, contact Tim Weaver at TWeaver@vcccd.edu.
