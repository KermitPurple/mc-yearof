Donald and Leeann Mulville have committed their support to their dear friend, Fr. Eugen (Catholic Missionary Priest) in providing educational opportunities to the children of the Bush Village of Kom in Mfumte Cameroon Africa. This personal mission started as wishful thinking that the children would have a working school and eventually led to a completed construction project of a school and church. Their project has now supplied over 300 families with individual solar electrical panels, and, they are continuing the project out of Kom Village and into outlying areas. The project continues with local fundraising and purchase/delivery of panels, as well as educational programs to teach construction of solar panels, recharging stations and ovens using local materials. Join Professor Mulville to learn more about her project; in addition, a panel of international students will share their own perspectives on wellness based on their experience in their home countries.

For more information, contact Stacey Chen at schen@vcccd.edu.
