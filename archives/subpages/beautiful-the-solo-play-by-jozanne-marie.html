How does one achieve wellness following a lifetime of uncertainty, sexual and physical violence, and emotional trauma? How does one overcome the pain of shattered dreams and betrayal? See how one girl took her power back and refused to be a victim. Beautiful is a solo play poetically conveyed with persuasive narrative and dialogue. Come and witness the resiliency of the human spirit portrayed in this remarkable play.

It is an incredibly moving story of bravery, survival, and victory.

Beautiful is a true story about a girl, an island and a secret. As we celebrate the Year of Wellness, this story takes us on a journey to healing and health. Come and be a part of the audience and witness a portrayal of strength and determination of one person to overcome adversity and victimization in this award-winning play.
