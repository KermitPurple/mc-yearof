The Future of Food

29

JAN 2013

February 5, 2013 – Room # FH-114 (Fountain Hall)

1:00 PM – 2:45 PM The Future of Food, 2004, is a critical look at North American farming, food production, and genetically modified foods.

2:45 – 3:30 PM A lecture and discussion on the The Future of Food and the pros and cons of bioengineered food by Dr. Subash Karkare, Professor of Biology and Biotechnology, Moorpark College.

Topics raised through this film series and related discourses include:

  • The Future of Food Production
  • The Pros and Cons of Bioengineered Food
  • The Ethics of Food Choices
  • Human Rights Abuses in the Cultivation and Harvesting of Food
