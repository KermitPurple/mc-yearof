“Coming Back”: Military Veterans Transitioning Home & How Civilians Can Support Them

16

APR 2016

April 21, 2016
1:00pm – 2:30pm at PS 202

Join us in watching a PBS episode of “Coming Back With Wes Moore.” This fantastic series explores the experience of military veterans and their transition home. Additionally, we will explore how civilians can support them as they adjust to their new lives. With guests from the Health Center, Moorpark College Faculty, and the Veterans Resource Center, we will explore important questions regarding both the veteran and civilian experience. As a veteran, how do you fit into a world that has changed during your absence? Is that world accepting of how you’ve changed? What are veteran needs? How do faculty and staff make sure veteran needs are met? What kind of resources does Moorpark College have regarding these issues? Please join us and support our veteran community.

For more information, contact Sabrina Jiminez at sjiminez@vcccd.edu.
