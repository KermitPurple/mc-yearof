Digital Labor: Technology Will Set You Free?

24

SEP 2013

Tuesday, September 24, 2013 1:00PM-3:00PM
EATM 208

This panel follows the screening of “Sleep Dealer” by Alex Rivera, a dystopian science fiction film, exploring our relationship with technology. Have we created liberation or enslavement? Facilitated by professors Andrea Ehrgott, Rick Feilden, and Scarlet Relle. We invite you to join the discussion about a not so far-fetched futuristic world. For more information, contact Svetlana Kasalovic at skasalovic@vcccd.edu.
