The Flame, Fist and Dance Tour

28

SEP 2015

October 6, 2015
12:00pm – 2:00pm at Black Box Theatre

Lecture by Brian Burns

The Flame, Fist and Dance Tour focuses on healing from domestic and sexual abuse, dealing with the isolation immigrants or marginalized people might experience, and finding a strong individual voice. It combines the performance chops of:

  • Dora McQuaid, award-winning poet, activist, speaker and teacher, reading from her new book “The Scorched Earth.”
  • Pat Payne, nationally acclaimed performance artist, aka The Velvet Hammer.
  • Sandra Hunter, award-winning fiction writer and performer.

Dora’s and Pat’s work discusses issues that can be sensitive, counseling support from the Student Health Center, and Sharon Manakas will be present, along with another counselor. They will also provide contact information for support services.

For more information, contact Sandra Hunter at: tinyhuntress@roadrunner.com.
