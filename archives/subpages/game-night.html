Game Night

7

SEP 2012

We had a great time hosting “Game Night” here at Moorpark College. There was gameplay for sure. There was pizza up to our ears. But, more importantly, there was a lot of dialog in and around the issue of gaming in our society (the good and the bad), social games for change, and how games might impact the future of humanity. For instance, check out the below video. It’s a TedTalk given in which renowned game designer and educator, Jan McGonigal, speaks about how playing games (and playing them mor) will make the world better…
