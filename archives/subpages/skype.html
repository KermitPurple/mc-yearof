Skype with M.T. Anderson, the author of Feed

5

NOV 2013

Tuesday, November 5, 2013 10:30 am
EATM 208

In the Year of Technology and Humanity, this year’s One Campus, One Book selection “Feed” written by M.T. Anderson takes us into the world of a teenage dystopia in which everyone has information transmitted into the feed in their head. The book raises questions about humanity, technology, and the power of the individual. Author M.T. Anderson will discuss the book and do question and answer. For more information, contact Prof. Diane Scrofano at dscrofano@vcccd.edu.
