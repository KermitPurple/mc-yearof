NOV 2012

Thursday, November 15, 1:00pm, in EATM Auditorium

Guest Speaker, Jestlan Hopkins, Ph.D. will speak on the topics of Social Media and Data Mining

Presentation Title: The “You” You May Never Meet
The digital world and the physical world have merged. But how does that affect you? Over the course of this presentation, Jestlan Hopkins, Ph.D will explore some of the technologies and methods that are being used to create your self – both online and offline, with and without your consent. During this hour, he will explore themes ranging from social media to smartphones, from profiling to privacy.

Come learn and share how information and communication technologies affect you!
