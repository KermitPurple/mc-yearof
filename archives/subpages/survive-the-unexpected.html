Survive the Unexpected: Campus Emergency Preparedness Practical Training

19

OCT 2015

October 22, 2015
1:00pm – 2:30pm at Fountain Hall 215/218

Lecture by Kari Meyers

This special event for Moorpark College students, faculty and staff is a practical and interactive training lecture presented by the Moorpark College Criminal Justice faculty.

Are you prepared to respond in the event of a campus emergency? Do you know how to identify a potentially dangerous situation? Do you know what actions you should take during various campus emergencies? What do you see? What does it mean to you? How should you respond? When should you Run, Hide or Fight? To better prepare you to ‘Survive the Unexpected’, we will be walking around campus as part of the training discussion with an open question forum, site visits of our campus, and our expert suggestions and guidelines for practical survival in an emergency. Please join us in learning how to ‘Listen and Live’.

For more information, contact Kari Meyers at: kmeyers@vcccd.edu.
