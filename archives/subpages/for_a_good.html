For a Good Education: Avoid the Ivy League?

Are you having trouble Choosing a Major or Career? Are you having trouble deciding if you should transfer? Which University should you select? Should you earn an Associate Degree or Certificate? Bring your beliefs and queries to this interactive myth busting forum where you can present your questions and opinions about Choosing a Major and Transferring.

• Professor Paula Muñoz: Coordinator of Extended Opportunity Programs and Services (EOPS), Ventura College
• Moderator: Prof. Danita Redd, Counseling Department, Moorpark College
