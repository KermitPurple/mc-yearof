Let’s Talk About Sex

18

MAR 2016

March 10, 2016
1:00pm – 2:30pm at PS 202

Panel with Dena Stevens, Allison Case-Barton, and Chad Basile

“Let’s Talk About Sex” is a provocative discussion and power point presentation looking at the many aspects of sex and our roles as individuals, partners and members of a community. Sex is not solely a physical act. It encompasses our psyche, our emotions, our social interactions and our responsibilities to not only ourselves but to society. In an age where sex is at our fingertips 24 hours a day what was once considered “extreme” is now commonplace and today’s expectations of a “normal sexual relationship” have reached far beyond our own front doors. In this hour long presentation we will explore how the human experience is impacted by the many changes modern day and social media have brought about. We will also discuss how our choices and behaviors as bystanders contribute to sexual assault.

For more information, contact Allison Barton at abarton@vcccd.edu.
