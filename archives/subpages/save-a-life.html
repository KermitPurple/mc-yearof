Lecture by by Sharon Manakas and Dena Stevens

Suicide is the second leading cause of death for college students, and undiagnosed and untreated depression is the number one cause for suicide. You can make a difference, and save a life, by learning QPR (Question, Persuade, Refer), a training which offers hope and understanding to another individual in need. Individuals who participate in this training will be able to recognize early warning signs and clues, and how to communicate with an individual who may be thinking of taking their life by suicide.

For more information, contact Sharon Manakas at smanakas@vcccd.edu.
