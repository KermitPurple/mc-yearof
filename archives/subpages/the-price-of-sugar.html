The Price of Sugar

29

JAN 2013

Tuesday 1:00 – 2:30 January 29, 2013 – Room #: The Forum, AA-147 (Applied Arts)

The Price of Sugar, 2007, focuses on the work of Reverend Christopher Hartly, a priest mentored by Mother Theresa, who worked to improve the slave like conditions of Haitians unethically lured to work on sugar plantations in the Dominican Republic.
